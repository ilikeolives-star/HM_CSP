Arches National Park

Arches National park in Utah is a fun place to have a little staycation(or even people outside of utah). This park holds the densest concentration of natural arches found anywhere on the planet. There is around 2,000 arches in the park, some of these are anonymous while others are more like welcome signs.

Why should I go?

  1. Arches
  2. Hiking
  3. History/culture
  4. Nature

The Night Sky

Going to Arches National park will be a very life changing view. The light pollution there is basically nothing so youll be able to see the amazing night sky. So why don't you go on down and experience this view in Utah!
